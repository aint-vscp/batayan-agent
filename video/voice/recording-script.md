## Continuous read (just the words)

Ever been told you don't qualify for something — and never told why? Meet Batayan: a reasoning agent that decides what you're entitled to, and proves it. Built on Microsoft Foundry, with Foundry IQ.

Watch it reason. Liza asks if she qualifies for a scholarship. Batayan breaks the question into six atomic rules, and cites a receipt for each one. Verdict: eligible — and proven.

Mateo fails just one rule — the income cap. Batayan shows the receipt, the single fix, and refers him to a grant he *does* qualify for.

And Aisha left out her income. Batayan refuses to guess. It abstains, and tells her exactly what's missing. That refusal is the product.

It shows its work, and never bluffs. Batayan — because every answer deserves a basis.

## Recording tips (for a clean clone)
- Quiet room, phone/USB mic ~15 cm from your mouth, no fan/AC hum.
- One steady volume; don't trail off at line ends.
- A natural pace (~150 wpm). 1 good take is enough; XTTS needs only ~10–30s of clear speech.
- Export as **WAV** (mono or stereo, 24 kHz+). Save to `video/voice/reference.wav`.
