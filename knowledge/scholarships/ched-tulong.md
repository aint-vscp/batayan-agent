# CHED Tulong-Dunong Grant — Eligibility Rulebook (Illustrative)

## 1. Purpose

The Tulong-Dunong financial assistance grant helps Filipino students in recognized
higher education institutions stay enrolled. It is broader and more lenient than
competitive merit scholarships.

## 2. Citizenship

The applicant must be a Filipino citizen enrolled in the Philippines.

## 3. Enrollment

The applicant must be currently enrolled in a CHED-recognized higher education
institution. Proof of enrollment is required at disbursement.

## 4. Financial Need

The applicant's total annual household income must not exceed PHP 700,000. This cap
is intentionally higher than competitive scholarships so that more lower-to-middle
income students qualify.

## 5. Academic Standing

The applicant must maintain a General Weighted Average (GWA) of at least 80 out of
100 and must not have any failing grade in the most recent term.

## 6. Adjudication Notes

Each requirement above is decisive. The grant is non-competitive: an applicant who
satisfies every rule qualifies, subject to available slots.
