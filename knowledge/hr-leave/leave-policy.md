## 1. Purpose

This policy defines who is eligible for Acme Corp's paid parental leave benefit and
the conditions under which it is granted.

## 2. Employment Status

The employee must be a regular (permanent) employee. Probationary, contractual, and
agency-supplied workers are covered by separate arrangements and are out of scope.

## 3. Length of Service

The employee must have completed at least 12 months of continuous service with Acme
Corp as of the intended start date of the leave.

## 4. Caregiver Role

The employee must be a primary caregiver for the child for whom leave is requested.
Only one primary caregiver per child may claim the paid benefit.

## 5. Advance Notice

The employee must file the leave request at least 30 days before the intended start
date, except in cases of medical emergency documented by a physician.

## 6. Adjudication Notes

Each requirement above is decisive. Where the employee has not supplied the
information required to evaluate a rule, the request is deferred for clarification
rather than denied outright.
